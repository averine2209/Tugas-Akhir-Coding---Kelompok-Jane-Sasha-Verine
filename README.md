What is 2048?

2048 is a single-player puzzle game. Your goal in this game is to combine the numbers by sliding them until you gey 2048.

How to Play?

1. Slide the tiles: you can slide them up, down, left or right. When you swipe, all the tiles move in that direction.



2.Combine the matching tiles: two tiles with the same number will merge into the sum.

3.Keep combining to reach 2048: after every move a new tile will always appear, keep sliding and combining them to build bigger numbers up to 2048.What is 2048?
